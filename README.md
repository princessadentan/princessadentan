This GitHub is my **thinking portfolio**: a collection of AI market theses, product experiments, and research on how frontier technologies move from technical capability to real-world adoption, especially in markets that are often an afterthought in Silicon Valley conversations.

## What I focus on

- **AI product and commercialization** -- where value accrues, what users actually adopt, and which layers of the stack become durable
